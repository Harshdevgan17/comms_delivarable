INCLUSION AND DIVERSITY

Advocating

Advocating for inclusion involves actively promoting the importance of creating communication environments where everyone feels welcome and valued, regardless of their background or identity.

Advocating for diversity entails championing the representation of diverse voices and perspectives in communication processes, ensuring that all individuals are included and heard.

Participating

Participating in inclusion efforts involves actively engaging individuals from diverse backgrounds in communication activities and decision-making processes, fostering a sense of belonging and ownership.

Participating in diversity initiatives entails collaborating with diverse stakeholders to ensure that their perspectives and experiences are included in communication planning and execution.

Planning

Planning with inclusion involves developing communication strategies that prioritize creating spaces and opportunities for individuals of all backgrounds to participate and contribute.

Planning for diversity requires considering the diverse needs and preferences of stakeholders when designing communication materials and platforms, ensuring that they resonate with a wide range of audiences.

EQUITY AND ACCESSIBILITY

Advocating

Advocating for equity involves actively promoting fairness and justice in communication practices, ensuring that everyone's voice is heard and valued.

Advocating for accessibility entails raising awareness about the importance of making communication channels and materials accessible to individuals with diverse needs.

Clarifying

Clarifying equity requires providing clear explanations of policies and practices aimed at ensuring fairness and impartiality in communication processes.

Clarifying accessibility involves making information easily understandable and available to individuals with disabilities, using plain language and alternative formats as needed.

Setting Goals

Setting goals for equity involves establishing objectives to promote diversity, inclusion, and fairness in communication practices and outcomes.

Setting goals for accessibility entails defining targets for improving the accessibility of communication materials, platforms, and environments to enhance inclusivity for individuals with disabilities.
